ABOUT THE COMPANY

Mission

The mission of #SpaceWithinReach is to make it easy for anyone, ranging from individuals to universities, to launch experiments and explore the earth at altitudes of 100,000+ feet using a space balloon. The SpaceWithinReach movement is exactly as it sounds, we're bringing space to everyone. Through a developed and thouroughly tested kit, Shepherd Space Systems will provide the necessities to launch and recover a space balloon.

The System

This system has a few main components. The first item is the balloon, which is responsible for carrying the payload to altitudes in excess of 100,000ft. The next component is the payload, this is the part of the system that will allow you to collect and receive data from your balloon. The next component is a recovery system, which will allow your balloon to travel back to earth without harm. The next component is the receiver. The receiver is responsible for collecting the data from the balloon payload. The final component is the user interface, responsible for reading the data from the receiver. All of these systems are neccessary to provide you with the ability to launch a payload to near space.
